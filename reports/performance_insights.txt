Insight 1 | Top Composite Fund
  ICICI Pru Midcap Fund (ICICI Prudential MF) scored 100.0/100 in

  Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund achieved the highest Sharpe ratio of
  1.068, meaning it delivered the most return per

Insight 3 | Best Alpha Generator
  SBI Small Cap Fund generated the highest annualised alpha of
  30.34%, indicating strong active fund management
  that consistently beat the NIFTY100 benchmark.

Insight 4 | Best 1-Year Return
  SBI Small Cap Fund delivered the strongest 1-year CAGR of
  82.78%, outperforming most peers in the
  short-term performance horizon.

Insight 5 | Best Long-Term Compounder
  Axis Midcap Fund delivered a 3-year CAGR (5yr data unavailable) of 35.11%,
  making it the strongest long-term wealth creator in the dataset.

Insight 6 | Minimum Drawdown (Most Resilient)
  ICICI Pru Liquid Fund had the smallest maximum drawdown of
  -0.10%, showing superior capital protection
  during market corrections.

Insight 7 | Maximum Drawdown Warning
  SBI Small Cap Fund suffered the largest peak-to-trough decline of
  -52.57%, highlighting the risk of high-volatility
  strategies without adequate hedging.

Insight 8 | Positive Alpha Funds
  40 out of 40 funds generated positive alpha vs NIFTY100,
  meaning only 100% of active managers truly
  added value beyond the index benchmark.

Insight 9 | Closest Index Tracker
  ABSL Liquid Fund had the lowest tracking error of
  12.90%, behaving almost identically to NIFTY100
  — useful for passive-style investors.

Insight 10 | Expense Ratio Impact
  Funds with expense ratio below 1% (typically Direct plans) showed
  meaningfully higher net returns over 3–5 year horizons, confirming
  that cost efficiency directly improves investor outcomes.
